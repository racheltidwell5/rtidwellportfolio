Smoked Gouda Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

Instructions

Measurement Ingredient
1 LB Short pasta, elbow macaroni or shells
16 Ounces Smoked gouda cheese, grated and divided
8 Ounces Extra Sharp White Cheddar Cheese, grated
1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter
4 Tablespoons All-Purpose Flour
2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
2 Cups Whole Milk
1/2 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
1/2 Teaspoon Ground White Pepper
1/2 Teaspoon Ground Mustard
1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
1/4 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
4 Slices Bacon
  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions, al dente, and then set aside.
  3. In a large dutch oven or pot, heat butter over medium-low heat.
    Once butter has melted, add in flour, and whisk until all flour disappears.
  4. Then slowly add in heavy cream and milk. Stir and let mixture come up to a slight boil.
    Add in all spices and stir again.
  5. Lastly, add in 8-ounces of smoked gouda cheese and white cheddar cheese.
    Whisk until all cheese has melted.
